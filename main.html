Anime Show List

Apothecary Diaries

Maomao, a sharp-witted apothecary uses her keen intellect and knowledge of medicine to solve mysterious incidents within the emperor's palace.

Attack on Titan

Humans fight for survival against giant humanoid Titans in a post-apocalyptic world.

One Piece

Follow Monkey D. Luffy and his pirate crew in order to find the greatest treasure ever left by the legendary Pirate, Gold Roger.

My Hero Academia

A boy born without superpowers in a world where they are the norm but still dreams of becoming a hero.

Frieren

The elven mage Frieren embarks on a journey to understand human emotions and the fleeting nature of time after the death of her companions.

Black Clover

Asta, a boy born without magic, as he strives to become the Wizard King in a magical world.

Spy X Family

A spy, an assassin, and a telepath who form a fake family to complete their secret missions.

That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime

Satoru, who is reincarnated as a slime in a fantasy world, gaining extraordinary powers and building a city for monsters.

Delicious in Dungeon

A group of adventurers who explore a dungeon, cooking and eating monsters they encounter to survive and continue their quest.

Fullmetal Alchemist

Brothers Edward and Alphonse Elric, who use alchemy to search for the Philosopher’s Stone after a failed experiment costs them dearly.

Death Note

Light Yagami, a high school student who discovers a mysterious notebook that allows him to kill anyone by writing their name in it, sparking a dangerous game of cat and mouse with a brilliant detective named L.

Komi Can't Communicate

Shoko Komi, a high school girl with extreme social anxiety, as she strives to make 100 friends with the help of her classmate, Tadano.

Bubble

A post-apocalyptic Tokyo where gravity-defying bubbles have altered reality. It follows Hibiki, a skilled parkour athlete, and Uta, a mysterious girl, as they form a bond and uncover the secrets behind the bubbles while navigating this surreal world.

Solo Leveling

Jinwoo Sung, a weak hunter in a world of monsters and magic, who gains the unique ability to level up endlessly and becomes the strongest hunter.

Arrietty

A tiny borrower named Arrietty, who lives hidden in a human household, as she forms a secret friendship with a boy named Sho, while trying to protect her family's existence from being discovered.

Porco Rosso

An Italian World War I flying ace who has been mysteriously transformed into a pig, as he navigates adventures as a bounty hunter, fighting sky pirates and reflecting on his past.

Your Name

Two teenagers, Taki and Mitsuha, who mysteriously swap bodies, leading them on a journey to connect across time and uncover a deep, life-changing connection.

Howl's Moving Castle

Sophie, a young woman cursed into an elderly body, as she seeks refuge with the mysterious wizard Howl in his magical, moving castle, embarking on a journey of love, self-discovery, and breaking the spell.

Rising Impact

Gawain, a young boy with extraordinary strength and passion for golf, as he enters the world of competitive golf and challenges stronger opponents to become the greatest golfer.

Wakfu

Yugo, a young boy with mysterious powers, as he embarks on an epic journey with his friends to discover his origins and save the world from dark forces.

My Neighbor Totoro

Two young sisters, Satsuki and Mei, who discover magical creatures in the forest near their new home, including the gentle giant Totoro, as they navigate family life and adventure.

Spirited Away

Chihiro, a young girl who stumbles into a magical world and must work in a mysterious bathhouse to save her parents and return to the human world.

Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind

Nausicaä, a compassionate princess in a post-apocalyptic world, as she seeks to stop a war between kingdoms and protect the environment from further destruction.

Avatar: The Last Airbender

Aang, the last Airbender and the Avatar, who must master all four elements—air, water, earth, and fire—to bring peace to a world torn by war.

Summer Wars

Kenji, a high school student who gets pulled into a family drama and a virtual world crisis, where he must stop an AI from wreaking havoc on both the digital and real worlds.

Belle

Suzu, a shy high school girl who becomes a global sensation in a virtual world as her avatar, Belle, while confronting her own insecurities and seeking to uncover the identity of a mysterious beast.

The Cat Returns

Haru, a young girl who saves a cat and is unexpectedly whisked away to the Cat Kingdom, where she must navigate strange adventures and find a way to return home.

Laputa: Castle in the Sky

Pazu and Sheeta, two young children who embark on a thrilling adventure to find the legendary floating castle of Laputa, while evading pirates and military forces seeking its secrets.

Ponyo

A young goldfish named Ponyo who dreams of becoming human. After befriending a boy named Sosuke, her wish comes true, but their friendship triggers magical events that threaten the balance of the natural world.

From Up on Poppy Hill

Umi and Shun, two high school students in 1960s Yokohama, as they work together to save their school's clubhouse from demolition while uncovering secrets about their pasts.

Transformers

The battle between the heroic Autobots, led by Optimus Prime, and the evil Decepticons, led by Megatron, as they fight for control over the AllSpark and the fate of Earth.

The Millionaire Detective: Balance Unlimited

Daisuke Kambe, an incredibly wealthy detective who solves cases using his vast fortune, and his partner Haru Kato, who disapproves of his extravagant methods, as they navigate crime and their contrasting ideologies.

The Garden of Words

Takao, a young aspiring shoemaker, and Yukari, a mysterious woman, as they form an unexpected bond through chance meetings in a beautiful garden during rainy days, exploring themes of loneliness and connection.

Cells at Work!

Anthropomorphized cells inside the human body, focusing on a hardworking red blood cell and a brave white blood cell as they work together to fight off infections and keep the body healthy.

Forest of Piano

Kai, a gifted boy who learns to play a magical piano in the forest, and his journey to become a world-class pianist, while forming a rivalry with Shuhei, a prodigious classical pianist.

Full-Time Magister

Mo Fan, a high school student who awakens to a world of magic and discovers he has the rare ability to control multiple elements, as he strives to become a powerful mage and protect those he loves.
